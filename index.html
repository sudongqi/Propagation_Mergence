The clustering method (Propagation Mergence) presented in this demo was designed to obtain community partitions in network which take both contents and structures into account. Popular algorithm such as louvain method (which only require structure information) was draw into comparsion. In conclusion, when contents of the nodes are available, Propagation Mergence will be a superior choice.
